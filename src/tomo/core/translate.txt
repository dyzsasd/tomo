Page 24: Challenges on the Flexible Operation

Even with a great performance in flexibility, it is still a major challenge faced by France's nuclear fleet because:
• Nuclear flexible operation demands are strongly influenced by renewable energy production, with new production records year after year: 17.8 GW of wind and 15 GW of solar in 2024 (Jan.-Oct.), compared with 17 GW of wind and 13 GW of solar in 2023, 15.6 GW of wind and 10.7 GW of solar in 2022. This trend continues in the future years.
• The April to August period remains the time of greatest need for flexible operations, but now more challenges are being encountered even during weekends in winter.
• The development of solar energy is leading to more and more weekday reductions around peak solar hours. The levers used so far have enabled us to get through the weekends. We need to work harder to get through the weekdays, which are harder to absorb, as some maintenance activities and periodic tests require full power production. We are building an optimized schedule for these activities to minimize impacts on overall flexible operation capacity.

灵活运营面临的挑战

即便在灵活性表现出色的情况下，法国的核电机队仍然面临重大挑战，原因如下：
• 核电的灵活运营需求受可再生能源生产的强烈影响，并且年复一年创下新的生产纪录：2024年（1月至10月）风能17.8吉瓦、太阳能15吉瓦，2023年风能17吉瓦、太阳能13吉瓦，2022年风能15.6吉瓦、太阳能10.7吉瓦。这一趋势将持续到未来几年。
• 4月至8月期间仍然是对灵活运营需求最大的时期，但现在即使是在冬季的周末，也遇到了更多挑战。
• 太阳能的发展导致了更多的工作日减少，特别是在太阳能峰值时段。到目前为止采取的措施使我们能够应对周末的情况。我们需要更加努力应对工作日的挑战，因为某些维护活动和定期测试要求全功率生产。我们正在为这些活动制定一个优化的时间表，以尽量减少对整体灵活运营能力的影响。
